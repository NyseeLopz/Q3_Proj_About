About Us

We are a community that help fund fire stations for supplies and salaries for fire fighters. In cases of fire-related accidents, we also help in charity drives for people who lost their homes. Education on fire preventions and precautions to take in dire need is our mission to create a safe fire-free environment. Extinguishing the flames (literally and figuratively yes) is what we do best!
